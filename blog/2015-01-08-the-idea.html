The Idea

01/08/2015

Since I'm a web developer, as a freelancer, I can work from any place that have a network connection. Fine! This year I'll travel across Europe! My goal is pretty simple: discover new places while working remotely.

Rules are simple:

  • Go to a new place for 4 to 15 days (maximum)
  • Book few days for work
  • For the rest, just enjoy your journey.

Why just 2 weeks?

I don't want to spend all my time traveling. I also like to be at home and enjoy my beautiful city (Lille, France). BUT, for now, I do want to visit a lot of cities. So here is the deal: traveling on short period, traveling often.
